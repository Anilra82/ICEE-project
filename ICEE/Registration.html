Registration and Conference Fees

Please read the following information carefully before registering to the ICEE 2012 conference.



                    Click here to access the Registration Form

                	

                    

                    
The conference fees will be collected by iNEER and, thus, the fees are defined in US dollars.


                    
After you have completed the registration form, you will be directed to iNEER payment system. Please note that you shall 
                    register and pay the conference fee simultaneously.

Refund policy

                      
                      
Refund requests will be accepted by iNEER only for payments made to iNEER payment website. Requests made to iNEER must be
                       consistent with the following cancellation policy, less applicable transaction fees associated with the refund:

                      

                    
    
                    
  • Before June 1, 2012 - Full Refund 
    • 
                    
  • Between June 1, 2012 and June 20, 2012 - 50% Refund 
    • 
                    
  • After June 20, 2012 - No Refund 
    • 
                    
  • All refunds will be made after the event takes place
