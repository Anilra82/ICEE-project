Social Events

In addition to the topical program and the conference materials, the full registration fee includes lunches, refreshments and social events. The social events form an important part of the ICEE 2012 experience and provide opportunities to discuss and network with colleagues and, naturally, enjoy the Finnish hospitality and local culture.


                 
                 
                 
                 Get-together Event: Most of the conference participants are expected to arrive in Turku during Monday, July 30. There will
                  be an opportunity to 
                 register oneself and get together with colleagues in the Brewery Restaurant Koulu that is well known of its own locally brewed beer. The restaurant, a former
                  compulsory school building, is located in Turku city center close to the conference hotels.
                 

                 

                 
                  
                  Welcome Reception: On Tuesday evening the City of Turku will [Aboa Vetus & Ars Nova] host a reception and welcome all the conference participants to Turku and Finland. 
                  The event will be arranged in the lobby and garden of the Aboa Vetus & Ars Nova Museum located in the very heart of the city.         
                  
                  

                  

                  
            
                  
                  
                  
                  Gala Dinner & iNEER Awards Ceremony:
The gala dinner will take place in Turku Castle on Wednesday evening. Turku Castle is a significant landmark at the mouth of 
                  Aura River. The castle is part of the Finnish cultural and architectural heritage as an architectural monument, a museum and as premises for distinguished celebrations. The castle is one
                   of the most favorite tourist attractions and most visited museums in Finland and it provides an excellent venue for a memorable conference dinner.
