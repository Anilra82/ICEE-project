Instructions to Authors
 
                    

                     
                   

                     
                     
Abstracts, full papers and copyright agreements are submitted using an EasyChair Author Account. The working language of the conference is English, i.e. the abstracts 
                     and full papers shall be written in English.



                     
Submission of abstracts, full papers and copyright agreements happens in two stages.

                      

                     
                        
    
                          
  1. 
                            
    Abstract submission
    
                          
    2. 
                          
    
                          
    Abstract submission was open until  February 10 (extended) and has been closed. The authors have been informed 
                          on the abstract review decisions. The accepted abstracts have been collected and saved for possible publication in a printed abstract book.
    
                          
    
                         
                          
    
                         
                          
  2. 
                            
    Full paper submission
    
                          
    3. 
                          
                          
    
                          
    Full paper submission via EasyChair was open until April 20 to authors of accepted abstracts and has been closed.
                           The peer review process is currently in progress, and the results will be announced by May 15 and the results have been communicated to the authors. A list of the accepted full papers can be found in the Program section of this website.
    
                           
    
                           
    If you have any questions dealing with the full papers or the peer review process, please contact the ICEE 2012 
                           International Program Committee by sending email to info@icee2012.fi.
    
                           
    
                           
    

                           
    The authors of accepted full papers can update their manuscripts based on the reviewers remarks via EasyChair until May 31.
    
                           
    
                           
    If a full paper is accepted and at least one of the authors has registered to the conference by June 15, 
                           the accepted abstract (as submitted by February 10, 2012) will be published in a printed abstract book and the full 
                           paper in an electronic conference proceedings publication.
