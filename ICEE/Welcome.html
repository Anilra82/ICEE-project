Welcome

The International Conference on Engineering Education 2012 (ICEE 2012) will be arranged in Turku, Finland. The main theme of the conference is Contributing to Success through Innovations in Engineering Education.


                
ICEE 2012 is the 2012 edition of the popular ICEE conference series sponsored around the world by the
                   iNEER Network (now with 38,000 members in 98 countries).

                   

                
These pages provide information on the event and help you to plan a successful trip to Finland.
